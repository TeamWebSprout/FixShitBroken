Moderation policy
🪪

Verified constituents post

Reading is open to everyone. Posting is limited to people verified to live in that member's state or district — one verified person, one voice. It keeps the board honest and hard to brigade.

🔗

Public & permanent

Every board and post gets a stable, Google-indexed URL that reporters can find and cite. The point is a record that doesn't get memory-holed — legitimate criticism stays put.

⚖️

Moderated in the open

Rules are published before the first post, not invented after an incident. Removed posts leave a visible tombstone, never a silent delete. Read the policy →

Opens in Phase 5

The boards aren't open for posting yet. Every member below already has a board — real member, real permanent URL — but they stay empty until verification and moderation are live. No fabricated posts, handles, or numbers will ever appear here. Until it's real, it stays empty.
